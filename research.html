My research explores the intersection of energy economics, electricity markets, and environmental policy. I focus on how infrastructure and policy design can accelerate sustainable energy transitions, particularly in the context of electric vehicles and renewable integration.

Working Papers

  • EV Charging Behavior and Infrastructure Policy – Empirical analysis of charging patterns and policy incentives.
  • Electricity Market Dynamics with Renewable Integration – Event-study evidence on market responses to renewable penetration.

Research in Progress

  • Demand-Side Management Field Experiments – Testing behavioral interventions for peak load reduction.
  • Policy Design for Clean Transportation – Evaluating subsidies and pricing mechanisms for EV adoption.
