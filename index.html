Mallika Sharma

PhD Candidate, Department of Economics
University of Calgary

I am an environmental economist specializing in energy and transportation economics, with a focus on electric vehicle charging infrastructure, household charging behavior, and demand-side management. My research combines advanced econometric methods with policy analysis to understand electricity markets and the integration of clean technologies.

With over three years of professional experience in research and applied data analysis, I bring expertise in both qualitative and quantitative methods, including econometric modeling and causal inference. My work contributes to evidence-based climate and energy policy, addressing challenges in renewable integration, transportation systems, and sustainable development.
